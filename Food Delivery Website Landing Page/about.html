Our Story


Online FoodStore was founded in 2020 by a group of food enthusiasts who wanted to bring the restaurant experience to people's homes. With backgrounds in culinary arts, hospitality, and technology, our team set out to create a platform that would connect customers with top-rated chefs and local restaurants, offering a wide range of cuisines and dining options.


Online FoodStore began as a small home-based kitchen that aimed to revolutionize the way people enjoy dining at home. We wanted to make restaurant-quality food accessible to everyone, regardless of location or dietary preference. Today, we continue to uphold our founding values of quality, convenience, and customer satisfaction, serving thousands of happy customers each week.
